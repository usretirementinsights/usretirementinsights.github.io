FAQ

Quick answers. Always confirm details with your custodian/provider.

How do I choose a Gold IRA company?

Start with the rollover process, then compare providers by total fees, custodian/depository used, buyback terms, and clarity. Avoid pressure tactics and confirm policies in writing.

Can I roll over a 401(k) into a Gold IRA?

Often, yes. Rollovers commonly move funds from a former employer 401(k) into a self-directed IRA. If you still work for the employer, eligibility depends on your plan rules, so confirm first.

What fees matter most?

Total annual cost (custodian + storage) and the dealer spread/markup. Ask for the full fee schedule in writing.

Fees & minimums (typical ranges)

Costs can vary depending on your custodian, storage option, account size, and current promotions. Always ask for the fee schedule in writing before funding.

  • Typical minimum to open: often discussed around $25,000 (some situations may be lower).
  • One-time setup: commonly around $50 (can vary).
  • Annual account/admin: commonly around $80–$125/year.
  • Annual storage: often around $100–$150/year depending on storage type.
  • Typical combined annual cost: often around $200–$300/year for account + storage.
  • Dealer spread/markup: varies by metal/product and timing (ask how it’s quoted).

Fees checklist (copy/paste)

You can copy/paste this into an email or call notes:

  • What is my exact minimum based on my account type and job status?
  • What are the all-in annual fees (custodian + storage) and are they fixed or tiered?
  • Is storage segregated or non-segregated, and what is the cost difference?
  • Which depository is used and what are the storage charges (annual + any extras)?
  • How is the spread quoted on the exact metals I’m buying (and can you show it in writing)?
  • Do promotions impact pricing/spreads, and are there any one-time vs ongoing discounts?
  • What are the steps if I want to sell later (fees, timelines, buyback policy details)?

Tip: ask them to email the fee schedule so you can compare providers apples-to-apples.

FAQ

Is Goldco a good fit for beginners?

It can be a fit if you want guidance through the rollover process, paperwork, and storage/custodian setup. If you prefer a DIY brokerage-style experience, a specialist-led provider may feel less flexible.

What fees should I expect with a precious metals IRA?

Common fee buckets include a one-time setup (sometimes), annual account/admin fees, annual storage fees, and dealer spread/markup that varies by product and provider. Always request the full fee schedule in writing.

How long does a 401(k) to Gold IRA rollover take?

Timing depends on your plan administrator and paperwork. A straightforward rollover can take a couple of weeks, but delays can happen when forms are incomplete or extra verification is required.

Do I store the metals at home?

Typically, precious metals in an IRA must be held through an approved custodian and storage arrangement. Ask the provider to explain storage options and costs clearly.

What should I ask before funding an account?

Ask about minimums for your situation, all-in annual fees (custodian + storage), the storage/depository type, how spreads are quoted, and whether promotions affect pricing.

How to choose the right Gold IRA company

  • Ask for the total fee list (setup, annual, storage, any transaction spreads).
  • Confirm which custodian and depository are used.
  • Understand the buyback process before you invest.
  • Avoid pressure tactics or “limited-time” claims.
